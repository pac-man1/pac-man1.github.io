Spritz (Butter) Cookies
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract, 2 Oz.
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (420 grams)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 cups butter (softened)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (200 grams)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Food coloring (optional)
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat oven to 350
    • In bowl, combine flour and baking powder.
    • In stand mixer, beat butter and sugar on high until light and fluffy, about 7 minutes, stopping occasionally to scrape down sides with spatula
    • Add egg, milk, vanilla extract; mix well.
    • Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture so it doesnâ€™t splash out everywhere; beat slowly until combined.
    • Do not chill. Fill cookie press with dough and with desired disks, press cookies onto ungreased cookie sheet.
    • Bake 10-12 minutes or until edges are light golden brown. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet on cooling rack. Remove from sheet; cool completely.
