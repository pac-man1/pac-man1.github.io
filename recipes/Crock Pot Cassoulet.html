Crock Pot Cassoulet
  • 2 Bacon Slices
  • 2 Cups Chopped Onion
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Rosemary
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 15 oz cans great northern beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 lb Lean Boneless Pork Loin, trimmed and cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1/2 lb sausage, sliced medium thick
  • Parmesan and Parsley to serve
    • Instructions:
    • Cook bacon in a large skillet over med high heat until crisp. Remove from pan.
    • Add onion, thyme, rosemary and garlic and saute 3 minutes until tender.
    • Stir in crumbled bacon, tomatoes, salt and pepper and bring to a boil. Remove from heat.
    • Place half of beans in a large bowl and mash until chunky.
    • Add remaining beans, sausage and pork and stir well.
    • Layer bean mixture and tomato mixture in alternating layers in crock pot.
    • Cook on low for 5 hours.
    • Serve with parmesan and parsley.
