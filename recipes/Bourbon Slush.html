Bourbon Slush
  • 4 Cups Strong Brewed Sweet Tea
  • 3 Cups Bourbon
  • 2 x12oz Lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • 1 x12 oz orange juice concentrate, thawed
    • Instructions:
    • In a very large capacity pitcher or bowl (1.5 gallon+), mix tea, bourbon and concentrates. Add 3 Quarts Water, and place in freezer for 8 hours. Stir, then freeze 2 more hours, up to 2 days. Take out of frezer 30 minutes and stir before serving.
