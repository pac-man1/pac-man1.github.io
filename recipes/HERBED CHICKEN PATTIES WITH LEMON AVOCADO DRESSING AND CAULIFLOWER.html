HERBED CHICKEN PATTIES WITH LEMON AVOCADO DRESSING AND CAULIFLOWER
  • 8 oz cauliflower
  • 1 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 3/4 tsp thyme
  • 1 avocado
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Cut cauliflower into bite-sized florets and place on a rimmed baking sheet. Toss with cumin and half the olive oil. Roast 15 to 20 minutes, or until tender.
    • Meanwhile, finely chop onion and cilantro leaves. Zest and juice lemon.
    • In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, onion, garlic, chopped cilantro, thyme, 2 tsp lemon zest and 1 tbsp juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then form into 4 patties per person.
    • Heat remaining olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add patties and cook until bottom is golden, about 5 minutes, then flip and cook an additional 2 to 3 minutes until no longer pink in the center.
    • Scoop out avocado flesh and add to a small bowl with another 1 tbsp lemon. Add salt and a drizzle of extra olive oil to taste, then mash until smooth. Taste and season with additional salt, pepper or lemon juice as needed. Spoon over patties and cauliflower. Garnish with additional cilantro as desired.
