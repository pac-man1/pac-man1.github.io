Sweet Potato Salad
  • [For Salad]
  • 7 to 8 cups Romaine lettuce or other
  • 1 large sweet potato, diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup roasted pepitas
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • [For Dressing]
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Handful Herbs (ex cilantro, parsley, chives, oregano)
  • 3 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper
    • Instructions:
    • Chop up stuff for salad, reserve some toppings
    • Mix salad dressing in food processor until it comes together
    • Dress salad in large bowl and mix
    • Top with reserved toppings
