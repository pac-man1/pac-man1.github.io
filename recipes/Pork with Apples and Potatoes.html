Pork with Apples and Potatoes
  • Canola Oil
  • 4 Russet Potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
  • Salt
  • Pork Tenderloin (Recommended is smoked bacon and black pepper smithfield)
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 4 Apples cored and chopped into 1 inch chunks (granny smith and fuji work well)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
    • Instructions:
    • Heat canola oil in cast iron skillet. Heat oven to 400.
    • Toss potatoes with salt and pepper to taste. Add to skillet on one side, and add pork to other.
    • Brown the pork on each side for 2 minutes, alternating sides with the potatoes halfway through.
    • When pork is fully browned, put it in the middle of the skillet with the potatoes to one side.
    • Scrape up the other side of the pan and add the apples and butter and stir. Once butter is melted, add honey and stir again.
    • Bake 15 minutes.
    • Add lemon juice over just apples, and zest over everything.
    • Allow to rest 2-3 minutes before eating.
