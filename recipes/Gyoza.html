Gyoza
  • Dumpling Wrappers
  • [For Filling]
  • 2/3 lbs Ground Pork (or beef)
  • 1/2 to 1 Napa Cabbage diced small
  • 1 leek diced
  • 2 Tbs Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tbs Canola Oil
  • 1 Tbs Soy Sauce
  • 3 Tbs Oyster Sauce
  • 1 Tbs Salt
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • [For Dipping Sauce]
  • 2 to 3 Tbs Soy Sauce
  • Dash of Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Chili flakes or oil to taste
    • Instructions:
    • Combine cabbage and salt and let rest
    • Combine meat with leek, sesame oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and garlic.
    • Squeeze excess water from cabbage and combine with meat mixture.
    • Place a small amount of meat mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper and fold after wetting edges with a dab of water to help seal the pouch.
    • Crimp edges together with dumpling press or by hand.
    • Dumplings can be frozen at this point. If freezing, LAY SEPARATELY ON A PAN SO THEY DONâ€™T FREEZE TOGETHER. After frozen, can combine into smaller container.
    • [to cook fresh or frozen dumplings]
    • Heat canola oil in a skillet.
    • Add dumplings and fry until browned on one side. Can brown all sides if you prefer.
    • Add 1/4 to 1/2 cup water and cover immediately.
    • Cook until all the water is gone, about 5 minutes.
    • Serve with dipping sauce.
    • Notes: Usually ~6 dumplings is a serving
