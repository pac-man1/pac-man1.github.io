Parmesan Crusted Chicken with Tomatoes and Green Beans
  • 1 1/4 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into tender-sized strips
  • 1/2 Cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1/4 C+2T Shaved Parmesan
  • 3/4 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1/4 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp Dried rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp Dried thyme
  • 5 Tbsp Olive Oil, Divided
  • 2 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb Green Beans, Trimmed
  • 1 Cup grape tomatoes
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    • Whisk together breadcrumbs, parmesan and herbs, plus salt and pepper to taste. Put in a shallow dish for breading.
    • In a separate dish, put 4T olive oil and garlic.
    • Bread chicken by dunking in olive oil, then breadcrumb mixture. Transfer to one side of the baking sheet. Spread remaining breadcrumbs over tenders when finished.
    • Toss green beans with olive oil and spread over the other side of the baking sheet.
    • Roast ~25 minutes, tossing beans halfway through and adding tomatoes at this point.
    • Garnish with parsley if desired.
