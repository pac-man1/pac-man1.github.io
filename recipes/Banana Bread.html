Banana Bread
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 to 3 bananas
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp bake soda
  • 1/2 salt
    • Instructions:
    • Whip banana very light
    • Butter and sugar blend
    • 2 egg one at time
    • ^add flour split salt soda mix slow
    • fold in bananas
    • 1 hour 350
