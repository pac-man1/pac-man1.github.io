Chicken and Tomato Cream Sauce Pasta
  • 1 Cup Roast Chicken
  • 1 Small Onion, Sliced
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Peas
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Cream
  • 1 tsp Capers, Roughly Chopped
  • Parsley to top
  • Cooked Pasta
    • Instructions:
    • Cook Pasta to taste.
    • Cook Chicken as needed.
    • Saute garlic and onion in olive oil until yummy.
    • Add chicken to heat through.
    • Stir in tomato paste.
    • Add cream and peas and simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.
    • Add capers and pasta over heat, and toss to coat (1-2 minutes).
