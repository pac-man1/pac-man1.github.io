Carrot Walnut Raisin Bread
  • 1 1/2 Cup AP Flour
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Cup granulated sugar
  • 2 Tbs granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Molasses
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 C shredded Carrot
  • 1 C raisins
  • 3/4 C chopped walnuts
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat oven to 350.
    • Combine wet ingredients in one bowl with a mixer. Combine dry ingredients in another (except carrot, raisin and walnuts).
    • Add carrot to wet ingredients and mix. Stir in raisins and walnuts.
    • Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir til combined.
    • Pour into 2 greased 8-inch loaf pans.
    • Bake for 60 minutes.
