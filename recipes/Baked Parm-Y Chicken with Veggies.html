Baked Parm-Y Chicken with Veggies
  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Broccoli Crowns
  • 4 Medium Red Potatoes
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 2 Teaspoons Black Pepper
  • 2 Teaspoons Onion Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Paprika
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Crushed
  • 1/3 Cup Parmesan Cheese
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat Oven to 400.
    • Cut broccoli and potatoes into pieces and spread on either side of a sheet tray.
    • Place chicken in the middle of the sheet tray.
    • Drizzle everything with Olive Oil.
    • Combine Salt, Pepper, Onion Powder and Paprika, and sprinkle over everything, including both sides of chicken.
    • Mix all together to coat with spices.
    • Rub Chicken with garlic and sprinkle with parmesan cheese (veggies too).
    • Bake 20-25 minutes.
