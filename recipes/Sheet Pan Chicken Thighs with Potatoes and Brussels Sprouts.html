Sheet Pan Chicken Thighs with Potatoes and Brussels Sprouts
  • 4 Large or 8 Small Bone In Skin On Chicken Thighs
  • 3 Tsp Dried Oregano, divided
  • 2 Tsp paprika, divided
  • 1 Tsp Sea Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Pepper
  • 8 oz baby red potatoes, halved or quartered
  • 8 oz brussels sprouts, havled or quartered
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • zest of one lemon
    • Instructions:
    • Preheat oven to 400.
    • Lightly oil baking sheet.
    • Season chicken with 2tsp Oregano, 1 tsp paprika, salt and pepper. Set aside.
    • Toss potatoes and brussels sprouts lightly with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice and zest, and remaining paprika and oregano. Place onto baking sheet in a single layer and top with chicken.
    • Roast for 25-30 minutes, then broil 2-3 minutes once chicken is fully cooked to brown the top.
