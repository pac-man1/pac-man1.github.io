Chickpea Salad Wraps
  • 1 15 oz can Chickpeas, Drained and Rinsed
  • 1/2 C plain Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 C Crumbled Feta Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Dill, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Finely Chopped
  • 1 tsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 4 9-10in Tortillas
  • 1 Cup Loosely Packed Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 Medium English Cucumber cut into sticks
  • 1 Cup Roasted Red Peppers
    • Instructions:
    • Place the beans in a bowl and smash until most are broken up.
    • Add yogurt, feta, dill, garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper and mix to combine.
    • Divide the mixture across the center of the tortillas, spread into a band across each tortilla.
    • Top with spinach, cucumber and peppers and wrap like a burrito.
