Dirt Cake
  • 1 package Oreos
  • 2 small packages instant vanilla pudding
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 stick margarine
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • Gummi worms to taste
  1. Chop oreos finely in a food processor and set aside
  2. Cream sugar, cream cheese, and margarine together
  3. add pudding and milk and mix thoroughly
  4. Fold in Cool Whip
  5. Layer in a flower pot, starting and ending with oreos
  6. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Fills 2 6 inch pots

Source: "Old Seidel Family Recipe"
