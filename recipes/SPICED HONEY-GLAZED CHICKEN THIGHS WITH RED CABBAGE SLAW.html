SPICED HONEY-GLAZED CHICKEN THIGHS WITH RED CABBAGE SLAW
  • 1/3 red cabbage
  • 1/3 red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
    • Instructions:
    • Place a rack in the top third of the oven. Preheat oven broiler on high. Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil.
    • Thinly slice red cabbage, red onion and cilantro leaves. Combine these with the remaining salt, 2 tbsp lime juice, and olive oil in a large bowl and toss well. Let it steep for 10 minutes in the fridge.
    • Meanwhile, combine chili powder, half the salt, cumin and paprika in a large bowl. Add chicken thigh and toss to coat. Place on the prepared baking sheet and broil 5 minutes on each side.
    • Combine honey and apple cider vinegar in a small bowl. Remove pan from oven and brush half of the honey mixture on chicken thigh. Broil 2 minutes. Flip chicken thigh and brush with the remaining honey mixture. Broil an 2 additional minutes, or until juices run clear and inside is no longer pink.
    • Enjoy chicken with slaw on the side.
