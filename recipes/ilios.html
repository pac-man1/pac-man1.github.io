Ilios
  • Honey Simple Syrup 1:1
  • Bitters
  • Cointreau
  • Bulleit Rye or Four Roses Bourbon
  • Orange Peel Garnish
  1. Coat bottom of glass with honey simple syrup
  2. Add three dashes bitters
  3. Add ice
  4. Add 1/4 oz cointreau
  5. Add 2 oz rye/bourbon
  6. Stir to mix, then twist an orange peel over top, rub around rim of glass, and drop in

Source: Inspired by Stella's Richmond
