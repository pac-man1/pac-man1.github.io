Blood Orange Cocktail
  • 1.5 oz Bourbon
  • 1 oz Blood Orange Juice
  • 1/2 oz Lemon or Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • 1 Dash Angostura Bitters
    • Instructions:
    • Shake with ice until cold. Strain into a glass with ice and garnish with blood orange slices.
