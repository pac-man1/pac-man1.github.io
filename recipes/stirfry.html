Stir Fry
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 package asian vegetables
  • handful of peanuts
  • 1 egg
  • any extra vegetables to taste (peas)
  • around 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • sesame oil, siracha to taste
  • Basmati rice
    • Instructions:
    • Cook rice following instructions on package
    • dice chicken
    • heat vegetable oil in wok
    • add chicken to hot oil. Be careful of splatter
    • Cook vegetables in microwave for 4 minutes
    • once chicken is brown, move to the side of the wok to continue cooking at lower heat
    • add vegetables to wok
    • add soy sauce, sesame oil, and siracha
    • make a hole for peanuts to cook in oil, add peanuts
    • make another hole, add egg. Let egg set for a minute then break up and mix into everything else
    • Serve over rice
