Rosti
  • 6 Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 German sausages
  • 1/2 of a yellow onion, diced
  • 2 strips bacon, diced tiny
  • 1/4 pound of Gruyere
  • butter
    • Instructions:
    • Boil potatoes for about 30 minutes until fork tender
    • cool potatoes in refrigerator. Can prepare hours in advance.
    • preheat oven to 375
    • in a pan, cook bacon, then dice. Save bacon grease
    • dice onion
    • peel and shred potatoes
    • In a large bowl, mix potatoes, onion, bacon, salt, and half of shredded cheese
    • In a small cast iron skillet, melt 1-2 tbsp butter and add bacon grease. Swirl to coat sides of pan.
    • Add potato etc. mix onto hot butter
    • Make room for sausage and cover with potatoes.
    • Cook in oven for at least 30 minutes+.
    • Cover with extra cheese and put under broiler until there's lots of brown bits.
